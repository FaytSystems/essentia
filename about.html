About Essentia

Better storage should be reusable, readable, and built for real households.

Essentia is for people who notice when herbs lose aroma, cereal goes soft, sugar clumps, flour absorbs the room, or carefully stored botanicals drift away from the feel they want.

$185.9k First-run target, including premium COGS, setup, freight and QA reserve, and $5,000 profit reserve.

The premise

A mason jar is trusted. It just needs a smarter cap.

Families already use mason jars because glass is inexpensive, reusable, visible, and familiar. Connoisseurs use them because they like control. Essentia builds on that behavior with a premium cap that regulates around the ingredient rather than treating every jar the same.

The product does not need to explain its private internal pathway to be useful. Customers only need to know that it reads the situation, protects the target, and avoids exchange when the room would make the jar worse.
