Why it exists

Most jars seal. Essentia manages the environment inside.

Food waste often starts with stale cereal, clumped sugar, faded herbs, and pantry jars that quietly drift away from the conditions their contents want. Essentia turns the jars you already trust into controlled storage.

Preset humidity profiles

Select herbs, rice, cereal, flour, sugar, coffee, tea, or legal flower where permitted.

Color touch LCD

A bright capacitive touch screen shows the selected profile, target RH, and manual override state at a glance.

Condition-aware exchange

Essentia only acts when the surrounding air can move the jar toward the selected target.

Premium rechargeable power

Estimated 4 to 7 months per charge from a protected name-brand lithium pack with USB-C charging.

Smarter than a sealed lid

It knows when outside air would help and when it would make things worse.

Set a target RH, then let Essentia compare the jar's condition with the room. If the jar is at 75% RH and the room is at 80% RH, the cap stays closed because an exchange would raise humidity instead of lowering it.

  • No packets to replace for normal use cases.
  • Manual override for unusual herbs, local climates, or personal preference.
  • Fits standard mason jars through regular and wide-mouth cap options.
