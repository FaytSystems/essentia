High-level control logic

It only acts when the room can improve the jar.

The public version is simple: Essentia compares the target, the jar condition, and the surrounding room. When the room would push the jar toward the target, it manages an exchange. When it would push the jar away, it waits.

1

Select a target

Choose a food or herb preset, or set a custom RH target from the LCD controls.

2

Compare conditions

Essentia evaluates whether the room is actually helpful before changing the jar environment.

3

Stay closed when needed

If the jar is 75% RH and the room is 80% RH, the cap stays closed because exchange would raise humidity.

Power estimate

Battery life planning, before final prototype validation.

A premium protected rechargeable lithium-ion or lithium-polymer pack that fits inside the cap while leaving room for airflow routing is expected to land around 2,500 to 3,000 mAh. Final runtime depends on display brightness, local climate, jar volume, and how often active humidity correction is needed.

Typical pantry use Estimated 4 to 7 months per charge with intermittent active cycles, low-power standby, and a larger premium cell.
Heavy cycling Estimated 8 to 12 weeks in homes with frequent room humidity swings or repeated lid interaction.
Quiet storage Estimated 7 months or more when the jar is near target and active correction is rare.
Charging USB-C rechargeable. Planning estimate is about 2 to 3 hours for a full charge.

These are planning estimates, not final certification claims. Final numbers should be updated after prototype bench testing.
